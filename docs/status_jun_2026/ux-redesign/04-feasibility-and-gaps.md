# 04 · Factibilidad y gaps

## Auditoría de factibilidad (AA / ADR / dependencias)

**Veredicto:** APPROVE WITH FIXES. Two blockers: most color proposals use a chart/pillar hue as TEXT, which fails AA on the bone canvas; and one elevation step hand-edits the vendored card.tsx primitive. Otherwise sound when composed at call sites. Minor items are bundled to fit the budget.

**Conflictos con ADRs:** vendored shadcn primitives: no hand-edit card/skeleton/status-badge; compose or regenerate via CLI; ADR-0011 WCAG-AA: chart/pillar hue as foreground text on bone fails AA; keep as tint/border/dot, use darkened avatar-N or pillar-strong for text; StatusBadge solid-fill invariant must hold; area (pillar) and status (state) color on separate elements; locked DashboardSummary and recent-activity schemas are ADR-gated; do not ship web consumer ahead of backend; ADR-0025: next.config.ts DevOps-owned; next-intl needs coordination plus a new UI-i18n ADR
